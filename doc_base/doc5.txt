Generic Name: clindamycin (KLIN-da-MYE-sin)
Brand Name: Cleocin
Treating vaginal infections caused by certain bacteria.
Clindamycin cream is a topical lincomycin antibiotic. It works by slowing the growth of certain bacteria.
