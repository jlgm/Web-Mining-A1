Generic Name: oxycodone/acetaminophen (OX-i-KOE-done/a-SEET-a-MIN-oh-fen)
Brand Name: Examples include Endocet and Percocet
Acetaminophen/oxycodone contains acetaminophen. Severe and sometimes fatal liver problems, including the need for liver transplant, have been reported with the use of acetaminophen. Most cases of these liver problems occurred in patients taking excessive doses of acetaminophen (more than 4,000 mg per day). Also, patients who developed these liver problems were often using more than 1 medicine that contained acetaminophen. Discuss any questions or concerns with your doctor.
Relieving moderate to moderately severe pain. Acetaminophen/oxycodone may also be used to treat other conditions as determined by your doctor.
Acetaminophen/oxycodone is a combination of a narcotic and an analgesic/antipyretic. It works in the brain and nervous system to decrease pain.
