Generic Name: acetaminophen/chlorpheniramine/phenylephrine (ah-seet-ah-MIN-oh-fen/klor-fen-EER-a-meen/fen-ill-EF-rin)
Brand Name: Examples include Pyrroxate and Dryphen
Relieving symptoms of colds, hay fever, and allergies such as headache, sinus pain, nasal and sinus congestion, sneezing, watery eyes, runny nose, fever, and itching of the nose or throat. It may also be used for other conditions as determined by your doctor.
Acetaminophen/chlorpheniramine/phenylephrine is an antihistamine, decongestant, and pain reliever combination. It works by blocking histamine, a substance in the body that causes sneezing, runny nose, and watery eyes. It also relieves nasal congestion and pain associated with sinus pressure, and dries the nose and chest.
